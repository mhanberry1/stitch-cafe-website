Welcome to Stitch Cafe

After a lot of difficult conversations and consideration, I have made the decision to close Stitch Cafe's current storefront this Wednesday, June 17, 2026. I know this news may feel sudden and unexpected. Truthfully, it was unexpected for me as well. I recently learned from my landlord that the space is being sold, and I needed to make a decision about the future of the business on a very short timeline. Over the past two years, Stitch Cafe has faced many challenges—from construction delays before opening, ongoing construction outside our storefront, equipment issues, and rising operating costs. Despite everything, this community has given me so much support, friendship, and encouragement. While this storefront is closing, I am working hard to keep Stitch Cafe alive and explore the possibility of relocating to a new space. To help make that possible, I have started a GoFundMe to support moving expenses, storage, and the costs of rebuilding in a new location. If Stitch Cafe has meant something to you, sharing the fundraiser, visiting before Wednesday, or simply sending words of support would mean the world to me. Thank you for being part of this journey and for helping create a community centered around creativity, fiber arts, and connection. ❤️
