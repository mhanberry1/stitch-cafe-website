About Us

Stitch Cafe is Capitol Hill's cozy hub for coffee, creativity, and connection. We bring together yarn lovers, crafters, and the community over specialty coffee, Sugimoto teas, curated yarn selections, and desserts exclusively by FogRose Atelier.

Whether you're here for a class, to explore local art, or simply to relax with a coffee, Stitch Cafe is a place to connect and be inspired.
